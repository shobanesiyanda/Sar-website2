About SAR

Shobane African Resources (SAR) is a mineral exploration and development company focused on coal, chrome, manganese and lithium resources across Southern Africa. SAR partners with investors and mining operators to unlock high‑value mineral assets.

Commodity Portfolio

Coal

Thermal and metallurgical coal projects supporting energy security.

Chrome

High‑grade chrome supplying global stainless steel markets.

Manganese

Strategic alloy metal used in steel production.

Lithium

Battery mineral supporting the global energy transition.
